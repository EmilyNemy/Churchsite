Where it all started

Chrisco Bugolobi was birthed from the big part of the whole church Christ co-workers of churches. The overseer of the church is Presbyter John Otaala, and his lovely wife Pastor Dorothy Otaala. The founding members of the church are Presbyter John Otaala, Pastor Dorothy Otaala, Samuel Otaala, Deborah Otaala, Joshua Otaala, and Catherine Otaala.

The Chrisco work is built on the five fold ministry stated in Ephesians 4 : 11-12. The work started in Kenya and has spread to various countries including Uganda, Kenya, Tanzania, Zambia, Nigeria, Sudan. In Uganda there are quite a number of churches. These include: Chrisco Luzira, Chrisco Bunamwaya, Chrisco Nansana, Chrisco Kasese, Chrisco Mbale, Chrisco Lira.
